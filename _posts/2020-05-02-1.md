## You're my everything.
<p>
There hasn't been a day where I stopped myself from caring about you.
I have not taken back my promises, to give you love and support in any form you need.
I planned ahead and also to support you. You have been a part of me and supported me.
<br>
But there are somedays when clouds go dark. Your mood changes. You change.
<br>
It devastates me.
<br>
But I smile and I keep going, because there is only one you in this world. My heart won't let go.
<br>
I will wait for you, please don't block.
</p>
